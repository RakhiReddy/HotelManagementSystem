eat
& drink
   The Promenade offers some of the best known dining and lounging options in Pondicherry. Lighthouse is a charming rooftop restaurant that gives the most amazing views of the majestic Bay of Bengal and is towered over by the town's ancient and now abandoned Lighthouse. The rooftop open air restaurant offers a tempting barbeque and grill menu for dinner.

   The Blueline is a fine, round the clock, restaurant that serves a wide range of authentic Oriental and Mediterranean dishes. Check out the indulgent and extravagant brunch buffets that Blueline is well known for.

   Risque, the exotic leather lounge with contemporary music overlooking the pool has a fine selection of international wines, mocktails and exclusive blends of coffee. Ask the bartender to make you a special drink from his repertoire of exotic cocktails.
