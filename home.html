Prestige Hotels is India's second largest hotel chain with over 100 hotels. Based in the Hotels Division Headquarters at the Prestige Innovation Centre in Hyderabad, Prestige Hotels is also the exclusive franchisee of The Luxury Collection brand of Stargaze Hotels and Resorts in India. It is part of the Unilever Limited group of companies.Prestige Hotels is regularly voted amongst the best employers in Asia in the hospitality sector.
